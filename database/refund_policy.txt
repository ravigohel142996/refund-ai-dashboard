REFUND POLICY - AI Customer Support Agent Store
================================================
Effective Date: June 1, 2026

1. REFUND WINDOW
   - Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the delivery date.
   - Requests made after 7 days from delivery will be automatically denied.

2. DELIVERY REQUIREMENT
   - The product must be marked as delivered to qualify for a refund.
   - Orders that have not yet been delivered are not eligible for a refund.

3. PRODUCT CONDITION RULES
   - NEW products are eligible for a full refund within the 7-day window.
   - DAMAGED products received from the store are eligible for a full refund at any point within the 7-day window.
   - USED products are NOT eligible for any refund, regardless of condition upon arrival.

4. DENIAL CONDITIONS
   - Request made more than 7 days after the delivery date.
   - Product is in "used" condition at the time of purchase.
   - Order has not yet been delivered.

5. REFUND PROCESS
   - Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
   - Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.

6. EXCEPTIONS
   - Platinum-level members may request a one-time courtesy refund outside the 7-day window per calendar year.
   - Gold-level members receive extended review for damaged items.
